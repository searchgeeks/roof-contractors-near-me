Minor issues like small leaks or a few missing shingles usually require roof repair, while widespread damage, repeated leaks, or an aging roof may need replacement. A professional inspection from a trusted roofing company helps determine the right solution.

Most roof repairs can be completed within a few hours, while full roof replacements usually take one to two days depending on the size of the roof, materials used, and weather conditions.

Can you handle emergency roof repairs after a storm?
